# Culture And Faith

Use this model for identity, acceptance, marriage/religious constraints, innovation/tradition strategy, holy sites, and why characters or realms behave differently.

## Mechanics Context

Culture and faith are durable identity domains in the save and major strategic context in CK3. The semantic claim ledger already supports culture and faith collection/domain names. Mechanics sources include `source:ck3-paradoxwikis-com:culture`, `source:ck3-paradoxwikis-com:faith`, `source:ck3-paradoxwikis-com:traditions`, `source:ck3-paradoxwikis-com:tenets`, and `source:ck3-paradoxwikis-com:doctrines`.

## Advisor Inspection

1. Resolve the played character's culture and faith names from the current save.
2. Compare those identities with local rulers, employers, contract targets, spouse candidates, faction environments, and travel destinations.
3. Inspect traditions, innovations, tenets, doctrines, and holy-site context only when the user question requires deeper mechanics explanation.
4. Keep mechanical advice version-scoped and cite documentation when interpreting benefits or restrictions.

## Advice Pattern

Culture/faith advice should make clear whether the issue is identity, opinion/acceptance, available mechanics, innovation progression, holy-site geography, or role-play direction.
