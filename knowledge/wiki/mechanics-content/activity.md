## Mechanics Notes

### Overview

- Activities are special occasions hosted by a certain character. Most activities have multiple variants to choose from. Each activity goes through a number of phases and during each phase there is a chance every 7 days for an event to happen. A character can only take part in one activity as a time and characters with the Incapable or a disease trait cannot host or join activities.

### Cost

- The base cost to host an activity depends on host's primary title. It is then modified by various cultural, religious and event modifiers, and culture era. Lastly, costs for activity options, travel options and caravan master are added. Hunt costs Herd instead of Gold if the character has Nomadic government.

### Wandering activities

- Wandering activities are only available to adult Dukes or above who have certain Wandering lifestyle perks. Inspection is also unlocked for all rulers with governor efficiency Administrative, Celestial, Ritsuryō, Meritocratic or Meritocratic Khanate government or whose faith has the Benevolent Governance tenet.

### Examinations

- Examinations are activities available to adult rulers who have Celestial, Meritocratic or Meritocratic Khanate government and can only be held in the realm capital. Activity guests can gain the Confucian Education trait or experience for it but the activity host cannot. Every character who takes an examination has an Exam Score, which determines their chance to pass the examination.
- Attempt an Intrigue challenge. If the challenge is failed the character will gain +40 Stress.
- Lover is present in the same activity they can be blamed but the relation will change to Rival. If none of these options are taken the character will fail the examination, gain -350 Prestige, -60 Merit and a Stress Level, and for the rest of their life they will have reduced Exam Score and certain event choices during examinations will become unavailable.

### University Visit

- University Visit is an activity available only to adults who do not have a tier 5 education trait and can only be held every 20 years. It can target any barony that has a university or holy building or is the capital of China. Constantinople also receives a bonus. If the barony's faith considers the character's faith Hostile the activity will cost 200 Piety.

### Funeral

- Funeral is an activity available only to rulers who have any dead relative they didn't use the activity on and can only be held every 5 years in any realm barony. It has a single activity option, Ceremonials, which affects the reward. When the activity starts it will give 25 Prestige. When the activity ends it will give the following rewards:
- +20 Legitimacy, +200 Piety and -20 Stress if Ceremonials is at level 1
- +50 Legitimacy, +250 Piety and -35 Stress if Ceremonials is at level 2
- +100 Legitimacy, +350 Piety and -60 Stress if Ceremonials is at level 3

### Camp Revelry

- Camp Revelry is an activity available only to adventurers which lasts 3 months. It has a base cost of 15 Gold and two activity options: Food determines the starting Carousing. Increasing it to level 2 or 3 requires a Supply Tent domicile building and can trigger events to pay Provisions and gain a either Piety or a follower.

### Coronation

- Coronation is a highly interactive activity that can only be held once. To have a coronation a ruler must be at least 12 years old, must have a Kingdom or above title rank, and if the realm has a Ceremonial Monarchy neither monarch can use the activity unless they are the same character. Rulers with Ritsuryō or Sōryō government can only use the activity if they are the top liege. Only adult characters can attend.
- If both values are at least at level 2 the dynasty will gain +50 Renown and if both values are at level 3 the dynasty will gain +100 Renown.
- Each Coronation activity has a Magnificence value, which ranges from 0 to 100 and is divided into 5 levels. The level reached at the end of the activity determines part of its rewards. After all guests arrive, each Coronation activity can have up to 3 Major Supporters and 3 Major Detractors.

### Oath

- When a Coronation activity ends the host will also have the option to swear an oath. Swearing an oath will add a decision that gives rewards if completed. The oath is canceled if the character loses its last Kingdom or higher title. 11 random oaths from the following list can be selected, provided the character meets the requirements.

### Blót

- Blót is an activity available only to rulers whose faith has the Gruesome Festivals tenet and can be held every 9 years. It can only be held in counties whose faith also has Gruesome Festivals tenet and how long the activity lasts depends on the size of the realm. The activity costs 200 Prestige in addition to its base cost and has two activity options: Festival Size determines Opinion bonuses.
- Sacrificing animals is free and gives 50 Piety and 30 Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm.
- Sacrificing majestic animals costs scaled Gold and gives 100 Piety and 30 Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm.
- Sacrificing humans is free and gives 100 Piety and 30 Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm but characters and counties whose faith does not have the Gruesome Festivals or Human Sacrifice tenets will lose 50 Opinion for 5 years.
- Sacrificing majestic humans costs scaled Gold and gives 250 Piety and 30 Opinion for 5 years with every character of the same faith in the realm but characters and counties whose faith does not have the Gruesome Festivals or Human Sacrifice tenets will lose 50 Opinion.

### Chariot Race

- Chariot Race is a highly interactive activity that can be held every 10 years in the realm capital. It is only available for adult Emperors and above whose culture has the Roman Ceremonies tradition and is more succesful if the realm capital is Constantinople. The activity has one Activity Option: Spectacles.

### Grand Tournament

- Grand Tournament is a highly interactive activity that can be held every 10 years in a Domain barony. It is only available for adult characters. The activity will be paid with Treasury if the host's government uses the resource and with Gold otherwise. The activity has two Activity Options: Accommodations determine the likelihood of certain negative events. Can choose to pay 20, 60 or 180 Gold or Treasury.

### Contests

- A Grand Tournament is made up of between 1 and 5 Contests. Each character can choose whether to take part in a contest or merely observe. Taking part in a Contest grants the following rewards:
- Hastiluder trait or experience for it and gain a positive modifier for 5 years.
- The character in the 3rd place gains a small amount of Prestige. If they are an Accolade and the tournament had 4 or 5 contests their liege will gain a tiny amount of Prestige as well.
- The character in the 2nd place gains a moderate amount of Prestige. If they are an Accolade their liege will gain a small amount of Prestige as well.
- The character in the 1st place gains a large amount of Prestige, an artifact, and a stronger positive modifier for 5 years. If they are an Accolade their liege will gain a moderate amount of Prestige as well.

### Intents

- When a character joins an activity it must select an Intent, which has an influence on what events will happen during the activity and can be changed at any point.
