## Mechanics Notes

### Overview

- alliance An Alliance is an agreement between two characters that they can call upon or join each other's wars via a character interaction, cannot start or join factions against each other, and cannot revoke titles if one is a vassal unless guilty of a crime. Declaring war on an ally will add -25 Popular Opinion for 3 years.

### Creating an alliance

- Alliances are created automatically between two characters who are married. They are also created automatically when two close family members of the two rulers are married or betrothed to each other. ### Joining wars

- When a character is at war, each one of their allies will have the option to join the war via the Offer to Join War character interaction, which the targeted character will always accept. Alternatively, a character who is a war leader will have the option to ask their allies to join a war via the Call to War character interaction.

### Confederations

- A confederation is an alliance of independent Counts or Dukes who automatically join each other's defensive wars but not offensive ones. Rulers in a confederation cannot attack or raid each other, cannot create new titles, and their authority laws are restricted to level 2. Confederation realms use the map color of the ruler who created the confederation.

### Elevating confederations

- Once a confederation has at least 5 members, the member with the highest level of fame can unite the confederation into a single Kingdom. In case of a tie in level of fame the option is not possible. The option to unify the confederation is available in both the confederation and decisions tabs. It also costs 150 Prestige and requires 25 Opinion or more with every member of the confederation.
