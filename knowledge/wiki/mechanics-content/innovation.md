## Mechanics Notes

### Overview

- Innovations represent technological, legal and ideological advances that a [[mechanics-content/culture|Culture]] can make. Once an innovation is unlocked, it can be used by any [[mechanics-content/county|County]] and Character of the culture which unlocked it. Innovations are grouped into 4 categories: Military and Civic, Cultural, and Regional. The All Under Heaven DLC adds a fifth category - Silk Road.
- Military and Civic innovations have a small chance to progress towards being unlocked each month based on multiple factors, the most important being average Development in [[mechanics-content/county|Counties]] of the [[mechanics-content/culture|Culture]]. The second most important factor is the number of neighbor Counties that belong to another Culture which already unlocked an innovation (Exposure).
- The [[mechanics-content/culture#culture-head|Culture Head]] of each [[mechanics-content/culture|culture]] can choose one innovation at a time to be that culture's Fascination. The Fascination bonus is 20% as a base, plus 1% per the Culture Head's skill that matches the innovation's. With the Scientific perk, these numbers go up to 27% base and 1.35% per the Culture Head skill.
- Each culture will also have Exposure to one innovation at a time, picked from among innovations discovered by [[mechanics-content/culture|cultures]] adjacent and/or sharing religion with the culture. The innovation with Exposure is determined automatically, and cannot be controlled by any character. The Exposure bonus is 40%.
- Regional innovations are unlocked when the [[mechanics-content/culture|Culture]] has a sufficient presence in a certain Region, and remain unlocked even if said territory is lost.

### Eras

- Each [[mechanics-content/culture|Culture]] will be within one of 4 Eras which determine which innovations the Culture can unlock. Once at least 8 innovations from an Era are unlocked and the required minimum year is reached, the Culture will slowly progress to the next Era based on the average Development in the Culture's [[mechanics-content/county|counties]].

### Tribal innovations

- Unlocking 9 Civic or Military Tribal Innovations is required for the Adopt Feudal Ways and Adopt Clan Ways [[mechanics-content/decisions|decisions]].

### Civic innovations

- Civic innovations from the Tribal era have different effects depending on the character's [[mechanics-content/government|government]].

### Mural Sextant
