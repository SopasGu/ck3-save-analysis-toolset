## Mechanics Notes

### Overview

- The Royal Court represents a ruler's seat of majesty and is available to playable characters of King or higher rank. If the character has governor efficiency Administrative, Celestial, Meritocratic, Meritocratic Khanate or Ritsuryō government they must also be independent.

### DLC mechanics

- On the court screen, adult rulers who are not traveling or leading an army have the option to Hold Court every 5 years. Holding Court costs 100 Prestige for a King and 125 for an Emperor, triggers 3 random petition events and grants 6 grandeur Court Grandeur and 50 Legitimacy. An optional event will take place inside a Royal Court every 24-32 months, indicated by an icon above the involved characters.

### Court type

- Each ruler will have the option to choose between two court types based on the Culture's ethos. Court types determine some additional bonuses that the court will grant at certain levels of grandeur, as well as which courtier trait a courtier will gain after spending 5 years at a court with grandeur level 5. Changing the court type costs 500 Prestige.

### Tribal court

- Tribal and Wanua rulers only have access to the Tribal court type, which grants the following bonuses:
- +20% Army Loot Capacity and raid +20% Raid Speed
- In addition courtiers and guests will gain +1 Prowess once one year has passed, up to 3 times. Courtiers will gain the courtier trait of Warlike courts.

### Nomadic court

- Nomadic rulers only have access to the Nomadic court type, which grants the following bonuses:
- Level 2: loot +20% Army Loot Capacity and raid +20% Raid Speed
- Level 10: horde riders +2% Herd Conversion into Horde Riders
- In addition courtiers and guests will gain +1 Prowess and +5% Hunter Venator Trait Experience Gain once one year has passed, up to 3 times. Courtiers will gain the courtier trait of Warlike courts.

### Court language

- Court language is the language spoken by a court. If a ruler knows the same language as their court, the court will gain Court Grandeur based on each county in their realm that speaks the same language. This bonus is increased by +25% if the character's culture also uses the same language. If multiple royal courts use the same language, they will all gain additional Court Grandeur as well.

### Court amenities

- Court Amenities are monthly expenditures that increase a court's grandeur and grant various benefits. The cost of each level of amenities scales with the size and income of a ruler's realm as well as their culture's era and government. The level of Court Amenities can only be raised or lowered once per year. The last 3 levels require Feudal or Clan government.

### Sumptuary debate

- If Fashion court amenities are at level 1 or 2 and court type is not Tribal or Nomadic then the ruler unlocks the Accuse of Violating Sumptuary Law character interaction on adult direct vassals, courtiers or guests. It cannot be used on friends or characters who are naked from the Naked Priests special doctrine. The interaction will cause a combined Diplomacy, Stewardship and Attraction challenge.

### Court grandeur

- Court Grandeur is a measure of how famed a royal court is. Each court will have a baseline Court Grandeur, which is determined by dynasty legacies, court amenities and court artifacts. Court Grandeur will be raised or lowered towards the baseline at a rate of 0.2 per month, which is doubled if the ruler's dynasty unlocked the second Glory dynasty legacy.

### Expectations

- The court of Feudal and Clan rulers will be expected to reach a grandeur level that depends on their title rank and increases by 1 for every 40 counties they control. Exceeding the grandeur level will grant increasing bonuses while falling behind will give increasing penalties. (This table is incomplete. One can obtain bonuses of up to (and possibly more than) 40% lifestyle experience and 3.33 Monthly Renown)
