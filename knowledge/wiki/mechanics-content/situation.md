## Mechanics Notes

### Overview

- Situations are major historical incidents that involve more than one ruler and are happening over a period of time. Situations go through multiple phases, which determine how each ruler is affected by them.

### Natural disasters

- Natural disasters cannot happen in the first 10 years of a game. Afterwards, one of each natural disaster will take place every year. Selecting a disaster will display all affected counties with a flashing red border. Natural disasters are 2 times less likely to take place in the de jure capital of a Duchy, and 4 times less likely if it's also the de jure capital of a Kingdom.
- Warning phases last 40 days; halfway through, every affected ruler will receive the option to gain 10 Stress and enter a regency while the situation lasts so that recovery contributions will cost 10% less. If the ruler is already in a regency the ruler will not gain Stress.
- Impact phases last 30 days. Every affected ruler will lose 50 Legitimacy if not Hegemon of China and 100
- Legitimacy if Hegemon of China and every location will lose Control, will no longer increase in Development, and will receive a modifier based on the situation's severity for 25 years. Every 7 days characters will die, every army located in the situation region has a 25-75% chance to be damaged and lose supplies (depending on its size), and the rulers affected will receive a random negative event.
- Recovery phases last for 15 years and give every affected ruler -0.25 legitimacy Monthly Legitimacy. Affected rulers can pay various contributions to receive bonuses. The cost of each contribution depends on the number of counties affected, the severity of the disaster, the ruler's gold Tax income and the innovations from their culture.

### Flood

- Floods can only take place in counties adjacent to a major river. The major river can be any in the world but it is twice more likely to cause a flood if any adjacent barony has Farmlands or Plains terrain. Floods have the following levels of severity. They will always have a severity of 2 or 3 if the flooding river is Nile or Yellow River.

### Dynastic Cycle

- The Dynastic Cycle is present in all start dates. A character will become an interloper if it either owns de jure territory of Hegemony of China or they are located inside a barony that's both de jure part of Hegemony of China's and currently owned by them. They will become involved if they are either part of Hegemony of China or if they are a tributary who has Palace Access in their contract.

### Division

- The Division phase can be reached either through Catalysts during the Tension or Conquest phase, if the hegemon owns less than 30% of China's de jure territory or reaches the Illegitimate Ruler legitimacy level, or if the Hegemony of China title is destroyed.
- control 25 Control in every county de jure part of Hegemony of China
- vassal Tributaries with the capital in the de jure territory of Hegemony of China become vassals
- celestial Change government to Celestial if not already
- celestial Vassals that don't have Republic or Theocracy government change government to Celestial and gain Merit scaled by their age and skills

### Political movements

- In the Dynastic Cycle situation all subjects of China are divided among four sides, called Political Movements. Political Movements give various effects depending on the current era. A subject can change their Political Movement every 5 years at the cost of 250 Influence. The hegemon can favor a movement every 20 years at the cost of 250 Influence and 25 Progress towards Division.
- Every Political Movement has a Movement Power, which is the sum of Personal Movement Power of all members. Reaching 800 Movement Power unlocks for every member Dismiss Hook and Request Permission to Raid interactions and the Raid Estate scheme. Every Political Movement has a leader, who unlocks the Make Movement Petition decision.

### The Silk Road

- The Silk Road situation affects all rulers with their capital in one of the situation regions. There are 6 situation regions, connected in the following order: Occident < Transcaspia and India < Central Asia and Tibet < China. Every region has an innovation assigned and every 12 years a random innovation discovered by the Han culture will be assigned to the China situation and all existing innovations will move...
- +30 If a Grand Canals great project in completed in China
- +20 If a Grand Canals great project in completed in Central Asia or Tibet
- +10 If a Grand Canals great project in completed in India or Transcapia
- +15 If a Bazaars special building is constructed

### Steppe seasons

- The Great Steppe Steppe seasons are situations that affect characters with Nomadic government present in certain locations if The Great Steppe Game Rule was enabled. The character with the highest Herd involved in a situation will be considered the Gurkhan, who gains access to the Unrestricted Dominance law and the decisionEstablish Paiza System decision.
