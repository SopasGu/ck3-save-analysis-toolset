## Mechanics Notes

### Overview

- Travel is used for characters on the road who must leave their home for a time. The system is used by any [[mechanics-content/activity|activity]] that does not take place in the same [[mechanics-content/barony|barony]] as the character, as well as vassals who make the [[mechanics-content/decisions|decision]] to petition or pay homage to their liege. [[mechanics-content/adventurer|Adventurers]] also travel is also used while undertaking [[mechanics-content/adventurer#contracts|contracts]] or moving their camp.

### Mechanics

- Traveling has two important factors to keep track of:
- travel speed Speed determines how fast the caravan will move compared to [[mechanics-content/army|armies]]. At 100% the caravan will move at the same speed as armies.
- Safety reduces the chance of travel danger Danger events.
- Once a destination is selected a travel route will be created going through the shortest number of [[mechanics-content/barony|baronies]]. Detours can be manually added in order to avoid dangerous baronies or visit specific points of interest. While traveling, various events may occur.

### Points of interest

- When a travel is planned the map will display all points of interest, which are [[mechanics-content/barony|baronies]] that contain something noteworthy. Visiting a point of interest will give additional bonuses, which stacks if the barony meets multiple point of interest criteria. The following baronies are points of interest:
- Capitals of a Kingdom will give experience to the [[mechanics-content/lifestyle|lifestyle]] matching its [[mechanics-content/court|court]] type. If it does not have a [[mechanics-content/royal-court|royal court]] it will give
- Capitals of an Empire will give triple experience to the [[mechanics-content/lifestyle|lifestyle]] matching its [[mechanics-content/court|court]] type. If it does not have a [[mechanics-content/royal-court|royal court]] it will give
- Capitals of a ruler with an Illustrious or Mythical legend will give
- [[mechanics-content/holy-sites|Holy sites]] will give 100 [[mechanics-content/resources|Piety]]. If the character has the Wise Man/Woman [[mechanics-content/traits|trait]] it will also gain experience for it.

### Battles sites

- Battle sites are points of interest that will give [[mechanics-content/attributes|Martial]] [[mechanics-content/lifestyle|lifestyle]] experience and has a 20% chance to give a [[mechanics-content/traits|commander trait]] if the character does not have two or more. The following historical battle sites exist, provided the game is started after their historical year: New battle sites will appear following battles that meet at least 3 of the following criteria:
- One of the [[mechanics-content/warfare|war]] leaders, their heir or their consort/concubine was killed
- One of the [[mechanics-content/warfare|war]] leaders, their heir or their consort/concubine was captured
- Neither side has twice or more troops than the other
- 65% of one of the [[mechanics-content/army|armies]] became Fatal Casualties

### Travel options

- Each character who travels may select up to two [[mechanics-content/travel#travel-options|travel options]] to improve traveling. The [[mechanics-content/resources|Gold]] cost depends on the character's primary [[mechanics-content/titles|title]] rank. Having the Well-Prepared [[mechanics-content/lifestyle|lifestyle]] perk reduces the cost by 25%.
